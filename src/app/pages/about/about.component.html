About us

MetaVas

MetaVas is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), aimed at introducing a more accessible means to invest, develop and collaborate on community-owned metaverse lands and real estate together.

Our Organisation

In the MetaVas DAO, members can gain exposure to a diversified and growing portfolio of real estate assets in various metaverses. Holding a DAO token represents the partial ownership to the DAO’s treasury which consists of the assets as well as any future value generated from these assets.

Learn more

How does this work?

Our Governance structure ensures that the DAO is in a good position to achieve its vision while abiding to its core values: decentralization, transparency, inclusiveness and community empowerment.

The goal of the Governance structure is to achieve the above with a minimum hierarchical structure as well as maintaining transparency and equal involvement of DAO members, regardless of the size of their token holdings. All votes count.

All members of the DAO (who hold $VAS tokens) have the right to vote on proposals and matters put out for voting on Snapshot. Some members will also be elected to act as members of councils. In order to facilitate the operation of the DAO towards achieving its goals and wishes of the community, there are councils that have jurisdiction over certain essential functions. This system gives freedom to Investors, Creators, Developer and members. READ more on the Whitepaper.
